About My School

My school is the East Java "Veteran" National Development University which is located in Rungkut, Surabaya, East Java

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a superior university with a national defense character

Mission

1. Organizing and developing education with a national defense character

2. Improving research culture in the development of science and technology fields that are effective for the welfare of society

3. Organizing community service based on research and local wisdom
